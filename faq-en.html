Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about EnerTchad — a Chadian integrated oil company in formation.

What is EnerTchad S.A.?

An integrated Chadian oil company in formation — a public limited company under OHADA law, from exploration-production to distribution. One single company, not a group: no subsidiaries.

Does EnerTchad already produce oil?

No. The company is in formation; all capacities and figures are dated objectives, not assets in operation.

Where is the company based?

N’Djamena, Chad — Radisson Blu, Block D, Office 23, Sabangali.

What is EnerTchad’s capital?

A founding capital of 10M FCFA, with a targeted trajectory of about 1bn FCFA short term and 20bn FCFA long term.

Who can subscribe to the capital?

Qualified investors aligned with the project; the raise is structured with our investment partner GCIC.

How do I express investor interest?

Through the Investors page or by writing to contact@enertchad.td.

What are the eight poles?

Three form the petroleum chain — Upstream, Midstream, Downstream — and five support it: GreenTech, EnerTech, EnerTalents, EnerConseils, EnerChimie.

What is locally-sourced enhanced recovery (EOR)?

Techniques to get more from existing fields using chemical inputs produced in Chad (EnerChimie).

Does EnerTchad do renewables?

Yes, through GreenTech — on-site solar, flaring reduction and decarbonisation of our own operations.

What is EnerTalents?

The talent and training pole — “train before you extract”, building a majority-Chadian workforce.

What is EnerTchad’s ESG approach?

Three pillars — Environment, Social, Governance — embedded from day one, with dated targets and targeted standards (ISO, EITI, OHADA/IFRS).
