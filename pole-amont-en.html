Our approach

Frugal, integrated, sovereign: the Upstream method

Five principles guide every decision in this pole — from the first gigabyte of seismic to the last recovered barrel. A method built for a landlocked country, mature fields and carefully counted capital.

01

De-risk with data

Before any heavy commitment: re-interpretation of public data, targeted seismic where uncertainty is highest, reservoir modelling. Investment decisions rest on measured facts — never on a geological bet.

02

Target the low-capital barrel

Mature fields, marginal blocks, wells to re-enter: priority goes to barrels that can be reached quickly and at low CAPEX, at the pace of each funding round — no megaprojects, self-financing stages.

03

Integrate under one contract

From earthworks to the pump, eight OFS services and ten well-intervention lines under a single counterpart: fewer interfaces, faster mobilisation, a cost per barrel controlled end to end.

04

Produce with the country

Local inputs — natron, lime, gum arabic, neem —, Chadian teams trained through EnerTalents, national subcontracting: every barrel produced strengthens the economy around it.

05

Measure, learn, redeploy

Every pilot is instrumented: recovery factor, OPEX per barrel, HSE indicators. What works is replicated from well to well; what disappoints is stopped early, before it drains capital.

Services
DrillingDrilling engineering and supervision: AI-guided directional work, muds adapted to reactive clays, trajectories optimised to maximise reservoir contact.
ProductionContinuous well operation and surveillance: downhole and surface measurements, nodal analysis, optimisation of rates and reservoir pressure.

Diagnostics & optimisationWell logging, well testing, reservoir modelling and digital twin: diagnose before committing any heavy equipment.
Geosciences & seismicRe-interpretation of public data, targeted acquisition, geological models: subsurface uncertainty reduced before the spend.
Completion & testingCompletion design, sand control, production testing: the controlled junction between drilling and first oil.
Integrity & produced waterCorrosion monitoring, produced-water treatment and reinjection, discharge compliance across the life of the field.
