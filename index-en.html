Integrated petroleum company · Chad · in formation

Extract more, spend less, transform in Chad.

EnerTchad S.A. is an integrated, Chadian-owned petroleum company being established to build the whole value chain at home — from source rock to the pump. Clean energy, AI and modern enhanced recovery form a virtuous circle: lower costs to access more crude, and finance the country's energy sovereignty. A company in formation — figures shown are sector data or dated targets, not operating results.
